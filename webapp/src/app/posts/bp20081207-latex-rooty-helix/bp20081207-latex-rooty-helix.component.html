Latex Rooty Helix

The idea of the rooty-helix is very simple. One starts e.g. with the length of 1, adds a right angle with the length of 1 and the hypotenuse equals √2. If one continues with √2 repeating the procedure (adding a right angle with the length of 1) the hypotenuse is √3. And so on and so on. At some point due to the iterations some triangles are overpainted. Because of this, one repaints the overpainted triangles.

I'm quite proud that my rooty helix was considered by Havard Berland for wikipedia.
